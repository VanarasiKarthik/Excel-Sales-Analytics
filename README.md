### Purpose of Sales Analytics

Empower businesses to monitor, analyze, and evaluate sales performance effectively.

### Importance of Analyzing Sales Data

- Identify sales trends and patterns
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Improve business decision-making

### Role of Reports

- Determine effective customer discount strategies
- Support customer negotiations
- Identify potential expansion opportunities in high-performing markets
